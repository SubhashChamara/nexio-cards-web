Frequently Asked Questions

What is Nexio?

Nexio is a digital business card that helps you connect instantly through NFC and QR technology without the need for paper cards.

What are the advantages of using a Nexio digital business card?

Nexio Digital Business Cards offer several benefits over traditional paper cards.

Instant Sharing: Share your card via QR code, NFC, or link without carrying physical cards.
Always Up-to-Date: Update your info anytime, and recipients always see the latest version.
Cost Savings: No need to reprint when your details change.
Eco-Friendly: Reduce paper waste and your carbon footprint.
Rich Media: Add clickable links, social media, maps, or portfolios.
Professional Image: Show you’re modern, tech-savvy, and future-ready.
Analytics: Track views and engagement for better follow-ups.

Do others need an app to view my card?

No app required! Anyone can view your digital profile directly through their browser after scanning or tapping.

Can I update my information later?

Yes. You can update your details anytime, and your card will always stay up-to-date without reprinting.

What material are Nexio cards made of, and how durable are they?

Nexio cards are made from high-quality plastic (PVC). They are durable, water-resistant, and UV-resistant, making them suitable for everyday use.

Is Nexio eco-friendly?

Absolutely! Nexio replaces traditional paper cards, helping reduce waste and promoting sustainability.
